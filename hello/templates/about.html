Benefits To Society

Our aim is to make food available for everyone and reduce reduce food wastage. A lots of unsold, edible food is being thrown away. It's shameful to send unsold edible food to landfill, when lots of people are dying due to hunger and some are scavenging inedible food from bins and also this affects our environment negatively through the release of unnecessary greenhouse gases.

This new platform connects businesses having surplus food directly with consumers and social service programs in local communities.

1. Food Donation


Supermarkets, big retailers and restaurants can register and specify food type, quantity and best before date of food items they have available, and local non-profits will receive text alerts about new donations in their area. Registered charities and not-for profit organizations can register on our website to receive food donations. These organizations select donations that they're interested.

2. Special Discounts


Consumers can browse food items approaching their best before date, buy them at a discount and pick them up in store. Consumers are able to take advantage of healthier food items like meat and prepared meals that they would ordinarily deem too expensive.
